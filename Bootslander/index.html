About Core Point Ltd

Strategy | Transformation | Excellence | Performance | Leadership

Core Point Ltd is a strategic advisory and management consulting company dedicated to strengthening leadership, elevating organizational performance, and enabling sustainable growth. We partner with individuals, businesses, corporations, institutions, and government agencies to translate strategy into systematic execution and significant measurable impact.

In today’s rapidly evolving and highly competitive environment, organizations require more than well-defined strategies; they require clarity and the capability to execute with discipline. At Core Point Ltd, we combine strategic insight with practical execution to solve complex challenges, strengthen operational effectiveness, and build resilient institutions positioned for sustained success.

Our approach integrates strategic advisory, operational transformation, project leadership, and executive capability development to help clients navigate complexity, strengthen performance, and unlock sustainable growth and institutional excellence.

Dr. Andy Nkalari, PhD

Chief Operating Officer (COO)

"Known for analytical rigor, integrity, and solution-driven leadership that translates strategy into measurable and sustainable impact."
Overview

Dr. Andy Nkalari serves as the Chief Operating Officer (COO) of Core Point Ltd, bringing more than two decades of executive leadership, organizational development, and strategic management expertise to the company. A seasoned leader and accomplished management strategist, he is widely respected for his ability to drive operational excellence, strengthen institutional systems, and lead high-impact initiatives that deliver measurable and sustainable results.

Credentials

Dr. Nkalari holds a master’s degree in project management and a PhD in Management and is a member of the Institute of Management Consultants USA. His academic background, combined with extensive real-world experience, equips him with a unique blend of analytical rigor and practical insight. As a certified project manager, he has successfully guided complex projects, overseen large multidisciplinary teams, and implemented structures that enhance efficiency, accountability, and long term organizational growth.

Leadership Impact

Throughout his career, he has demonstrated exceptional capability in project governance, organizational transformation, change management, and strategic planning. Known for his analytical mindset and solution-driven leadership, Dr. Nkalari excels at diagnosing organizational challenges and designing strategies that inspire collective action. His leadership style emphasizes integrity, clarity of purpose, and the empowerment of people, qualities that have earned him admiration across the organizations he has served. A respected thought leader, he bridges the gap between theory and practice, applying research-driven insights into real-world business challenges.

Community Influence

Beyond his corporate achievements, he is honored with a traditional chieftaincy title, reflecting his cultural influence, community leadership, and commitment to societal advancement. In his role as Chief Operating Officer, Dr. Andy Nkalari continues to champion excellence, ethical leadership, and transformative growth, positioning Core Point Ltd for long term success in an evolving Nigerian business landscape.

Dr. Uduak Amasi, PhD, MBA, PMP, ITIL, CSPO, PCIP, MLO, FCISM

Chief Information Officer (CIO) | Senior Managing Partner

"She helps institutions translate vision into measurable outcomes, strengthen performance, and achieve sustainable growth."
Overview

Dr. Uduak Amasi is the Chief Information Officer (CIO) and Senior Managing Partner at Core Point Ltd., where she leads the firm’s technology strategy, transformation initiatives, and enterprise innovation agenda. She brings extensive experience in business and management consulting, technology leadership, and large-scale transformation across multiple sectors.

Expertise

A Certified Project Management Professional (PMP) and PCI Professional (PCIP), Dr. Uduak Amasi has led numerous complex IT initiatives, enterprise-wide software deployments, and strategic programs that drive operational performance and organizational effectiveness. Her expertise spans project, product, and program management, business process reengineering (BPR), treasury management, and quality assurance. She also brings valuable industry insight as an experienced mortgage professional with strong knowledge of lending operations and regulatory environments.

Advisory Contributions

Dr. Uduak Amasi advises organizations on strategic transformation, operational excellence, and leadership capability development, helping institutions translate vision into measurable outcomes, strengthen performance, and achieve sustainable growth.

Recognition And Personal Note

She serves as an Ambassador for the African Union 2063 Ambassadorial Assembly and is a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Information and Strategy Management (FCISM).

Beyond her professional work, Dr. Uduak Amasi is passionate about mentoring emerging leaders. She enjoys gardening and experimenting with new recipes in her kitchen.
