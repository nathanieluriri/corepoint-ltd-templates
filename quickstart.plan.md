# QuickStart Adaptation Plan

## Positioning Fit
- Use QuickStart as the cleanest and most accessible modern option for Core Point Ltd.
- Keep its soft, approachable, conversion-focused structure, but remove startup-product language and generic SaaS framing.
- Turn it into a concise consulting site that feels modern, credible, and easy to navigate.

## Template-Specific Direction
- Visual tone:
  bright, refined, and confident, with light backgrounds, strong typography, restrained accent color, and executive clarity.
- Design memory:
  a clear, elegant consulting homepage that feels fast, well-organized, and trustworthy.
- Constraint:
  retain the template's simple flow and adaptive friendliness, improving polish rather than overcomplicating it.
